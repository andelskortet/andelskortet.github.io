About Andelskortet

A community map made for curious cooperators

Andelskortet began as a volunteer project with a simple desire: make Denmark's cooperative landscape transparent, friendly, and fun. Today it brings together municipal data, cooperative filings, and crowd-sourced tips to help Copenhageners and Danes everywhere find their next home or membership with ease. Whether you're a first-time buyer seeking a foothold in the city, a family wanting to expand your options, or a cooperative veteran keen on comparing member perks, the map reveals opportunities you might otherwise miss. Smart filters, playful storytelling, and continuously refreshed insights make every exploration feel like opening a new door. Best of all, the experience is designed for both desktop strategists and mobile explorers, so you can dream, compare, and plan from wherever you are.
