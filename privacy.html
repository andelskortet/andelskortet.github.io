Privacy Policy

We believe transparency builds trust. This policy explains how Andelskortet handles personal data, analytic signals, and cooperative submissions across the platform.

Overview

Our approach to privacy

Andelskortet is operated from Copenhagen and follows EU GDPR principles. We only collect the minimum amount of information required to operate the cooperative map, respond to enquiries, and improve the user journey. Any references to ?we?, ?us?, or ?our? refer to the volunteers and partners running Andelskortet.

Data Collection

What we collect and why

We do not require users to create an account. When you browse the site we collect anonymised analytics events through Google Analytics 4 to understand which map features are most helpful. These events include device type, rough location derived from IP (city-level only), pages visited, and interactions with filters. IP addresses are truncated before storage, and no personally identifiable information (PII) is retained. If you contact us via email, we will keep your message, address, and any attachments solely for the purpose of handling your enquiry.

Cookies & Analytics

How tracking works on Andelskortet

Google Analytics 4 uses first-party cookies to differentiate visits and measure engagement. The cookies store anonymous identifiers such as `_ga` that help us understand return visits and session duration. We configured Google Analytics to anonymise IP addresses, disable advertising features, and retain events for a maximum of 14 months. You can opt out at any time by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by adjusting your browser privacy settings. We do not sell, rent, or share analytics data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

When we disclose information

We may share aggregated, anonymised insights with participating cooperatives to help them understand interest levels. We will only disclose personal data if required by Danish law, such as responding to a lawful government request or protecting against fraud or misuse. If we collaborate with service providers (for hosting, analytics, or email delivery), they are contractually bound to process data on our behalf and according to GDPR obligations.

Your Rights

How to exercise control

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. Send a request to andelskortet@gmail.com with the subject ?Data Request?. We will respond within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with the Danish Data Protection Agency if you believe we are mishandling data. Because we only capture minimal personal information, requests are typically processed quickly.

Updates

Policy changes and contact

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. When changes are significant we will post a notice on the homepage and adjust the ?Last updated? date below. Continued use of the map after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. For questions, please reach out to andelskortet@gmail.com.
